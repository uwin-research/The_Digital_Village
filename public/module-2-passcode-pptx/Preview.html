Confirm your phone actually requires a code/biometric to unlock.

 

How to check (simple test):

⚬Press the power button to turn the screen off

⚬Turn the screen back on

⚬Try to unlock

 

What you should see:

⚬A PIN/Passcode prompt

⚬Face ID / Fingerprint prompt (and it still asks for PIN sometimes)

⚬If it opens straight to home screen → screen lock is OFF (fix it next).

 

Turn on Passcode (Screen Lock)

Steps:

1.Open Settings

 

 

 

Turn on Passcode (Screen Lock)

 

Path: Settings → Face ID & Passcode → Turn Passcode On

 

Steps:

Turn on Passcode (Screen Lock)

 

Path: Settings → Face ID & Passcode → Turn Passcode On

 

Steps:

1.Open Settings

2.Tap Face ID & Passcode / Touch ID & Passcode

3.Tap Turn Passcode On

 

Turn on Passcode (Screen Lock)

 

Path: Settings → Face ID & Passcode → Turn Passcode On

 

Steps:

1.Open Settings

2.Tap Face ID & Passcode / Touch ID & Passcode

3.Tap Turn Passcode On

4.Choose a 6-digit passcode

 

Tip:  Avoid easy ones like 000000, 123456, or your birth year.
